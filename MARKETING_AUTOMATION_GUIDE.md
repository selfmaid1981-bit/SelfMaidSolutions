## ðŸ“± SOCIAL MEDIA AUTOMATION STRATEGY

Since direct API integrations for Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, etc. require business-level API access, here's the **best approach for small businesses**:

### Recommended Tools (Free & Paid Options):

#### **Option 1: Buffer (Recommended - Free Plan Available)**
**Best for:** Scheduling posts across multiple platforms

**Features:**
- Schedule posts to Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter
- Free plan: 3 social accounts, 10 scheduled posts at a time
- Paid plan ($6/month): Unlimited scheduled posts, analytics

**Setup:**
1. Sign up at buffer.com
2. Connect your social media accounts
3. Use the content templates from VIDEO_CONTENT_SCRIPTS.md and SEO_CONTENT_LIBRARY.md
4. Schedule 1-2 weeks of posts at once

**Workflow:**
- Sunday: Spend 1 hour scheduling the week's content
- Buffer auto-posts at optimal times
- Monitor engagement daily (5 minutes)

---

#### **Option 2: Meta Business Suite (Free - Facebook & Instagram)**
**Best for:** Facebook and Instagram only

**Features:**
- Schedule posts to Facebook and Instagram
- 100% free
- Built-in analytics
- Manage messages and comments

**Setup:**
1. Visit business.facebook.com
2. Connect your Facebook Page and Instagram account
3. Use "Create Post" and schedule for future dates

**Workflow:**
- Create posts in bulk
- Schedule for optimal times (see VIDEO_CONTENT_SCRIPTS.md for best times)
- Respond to comments and messages

---

#### **Option 3: Hootsuite (Paid - Most Comprehensive)**
**Best for:** Managing all platforms + analytics

**Features:**
- Schedule to all major platforms (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter, Pinterest)
- Team collaboration
- Advanced analytics
- Paid: $99/month (30-day free trial)

---

#### **Option 4: Zapier Automation (Recommended for Power Users)**
**Best for:** Automated workflows

**Example Automations:**
1. **New Blog Post â†’ Auto-Post to Social Media**
   - When you publish a blog post, Zapier auto-creates social posts

2. **Email Campaign â†’ LinkedIn Post**
   - When you send an email blast, Zapier creates a LinkedIn post

3. **New Google Review â†’ Thank You Email**
   - When someone leaves a review, auto-send thank you email

4. **Quote Request â†’ Add to Email List**
   - Already built into your website!

**Setup:**
- Sign up at zapier.com
- Connect your accounts (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, SendGrid)
- Use pre-built "Zaps" or create custom workflows
