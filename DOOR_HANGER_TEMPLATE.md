## WHERE TO DISTRIBUTE

### Strategy 1: "Neighbor Endorsement"
- **After you clean a house**, ask permission to hang door hangers on 10-20 nearby homes
- **Message**: "We just cleaned [Street Name]! Your neighbor loves us!"
- **Best ROI** - People trust their neighbors

### Strategy 2: Target Nice Neighborhoods
- Drive through upscale neighborhoods
- Look for:
  - Well-maintained homes
  - 2+ cars in driveway (busy families)
  - Toys in yard (families with kids)
  - Nice landscaping (they care about their property)

### Strategy 3: After Big Jobs
- Did a move-out clean? Hit every house on that street
- Message: "We just helped someone on your street move!"

### Strategy 4: Seasonal Targeting
- **Spring**: "Spring cleaning special!"
- **Summer**: "Too hot to clean?"
- **Fall**: "Back-to-school busy? We'll clean!"
- **Winter**: "Holiday hosting? We've got you!"

---

## TIMING

**Best Days**: Thursday-Saturday
**Best Time**: 10am-4pm
**Avoid**: 
- Early morning (people at work, won't see it)
- After dark (looks sketchy)
- Rainy days (hangers get wet)

## PRO TIPS

1. **Hang on DOOR, not mailbox** (mailbox is illegal!)
2. **Go with a friend** - Faster and safer
3. **Dress professionally** - Wear company shirt if you have one
4. **Be friendly** - If someone comes out, smile and introduce yourself
5. **Take before/after photos** of your work - Put real photos on the hanger
6. **Make QR code LARGE** - Easy to scan
7. **Weather-proof** - Use glossy cardstock or laminate
