## AD TESTING STRATEGY

### Week 1-2: Test Images
Run 3 ads with same copy, different images:
- Ad A: Before/after photos
- Ad B: Happy customer in clean home
- Ad C: Professional cleaner at work

**Winner**: Whichever gets most clicks at lowest cost

### Week 3-4: Test Copy
Run winner image with 3 different copy versions:
- Copy A: Feature-focused (insured, experience, etc.)
- Copy B: Benefit-focused (save time, enjoy life, etc.)
- Copy C: Offer-focused ($20 off, free quote, etc.)

**Winner**: Whichever gets most conversions

### Month 2+: Scale winner!

## FACEBOOK AD SETUP GUIDE

### Step 1: Create Business Page
(You may already have this!)
- facebook.com/pages/create
- Add logo, cover photo, business info
- Post 5-10 times before running ads (looks more legit)

### Step 2: Set Up Ads Manager
- business.facebook.com
- Create ad account
- Add payment method

### Step 3: Create Campaign
1. **Objective**: "Leads" or "Messages"
2. **Budget**: $5/day
3. **Audience**: 
   - Location: Montgomery, Prattville, Selma
   - Age: 30-65
   - Detailed targeting: Homeowners, parents, etc.
4. **Placements**: Facebook Feed + Instagram Feed (uncheck everything else)
5. **Ad creative**: Upload image + copy from above

### Step 4: Create Lead Form
**Question 1**: Name
**Question 2**: Phone number
**Question 3**: Email
**Question 4**: Service interested in (dropdown)
**Question 5**: Preferred date (optional)

### Step 5: Launch!

## COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

‚ùå Too much text on images (Facebook penalizes this)
‚ùå Targeting too broad (all of Alabama = waste of money)
‚ùå Not using lead forms (making people call/visit website loses 70%)
‚ùå Giving up after 1 week (needs time to optimize)
‚ùå Not responding fast to leads (respond within 1 hour!)

## WINNING AD FORMULA

1. **Attention-grabbing image** (before/after works best)
2. **Clear benefit** (save time, get deposit back, etc.)
3. **Trust signals** (16 years, insured, 500+ customers)
4. **Clear offer** ($20 off or free quote)
5. **Easy action** (call or fill form)

---

## RESPONSE PROTOCOL

When you get a lead:

**Within 1 hour**:
1. Call them
2. If no answer, text: "Hi [Name]! Got your request for a cleaning quote. When's a good time to chat? - Self-Maid Cleaning"
3. Send email with quote form link

**Within 24 hours**: Follow up again if no response

**Conversion rate drops 80% if you wait more than 24 hours!**
